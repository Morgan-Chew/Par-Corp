About Us

PAR Corporation SHD BHD

PAR Corporation SDN BHD is a local company incorporated in Brunei Darussalam on November 2005 and is based in Bandar Seri Begawan. The incorporation of PAR Corporation SDN BHD is in line with the Brunei Governments call for more local participation to support the Small and Medium Enterprise Policy (SME) in the country's economy.

Fueled by our group of experienced and dedicated management personnel, our company operates through various departments designed to ensure effective management of the company in order for us to provide quality and satisfactory services to all our reputable clients..

With years of industry experience. PAR Corporation is Specialized in the Wireless Solution, Telecommunicationsand IT Industry.

Why Choose PAR?

PAR Corporation Sdn Bhd has been actively involved in the following category of services as the Supplier, Provider and Contractor for various sectors, such as the ICT Sector, Mechanical, Electrical & Instrumentation Services Sector, Specialist Works & Services Sector and Civil & Structural, and Building Contractor.

Our company's ability to adapt to changes and diversification approach and its competitive advantage with using the latest technologies available lives in its ability to offer our reputable clients and total services in solving and assisting solution.
