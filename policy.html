HEALTH, SAFETY AND ENVIRONMENTAL (HSE) POLICY STATEMENT

It is the policy of PAR CORPORATION SDN BHD to undertake their activities, such that the health and safety of their employees and all other persons is given due priority, and also that the conservation of the environment is reflected is in those activities.

PAR CORPORATION SDN BHD, in implementing this policy, will, in addition to complying with relevant legislation, ensure that effective measure are employed to provide the necessary protection for the safety, health and environment of those who, directly or indirectly, may be effected by PAR CORPORATION SDN BHD activities.

This commitment by PAR CORPORATION SDN BHD will include the following provisions:

  • Ensure employees and relevant authorities are aware of potential hazards that may affect them, and the action being taken to minimize the risk involved.
  • Ensure that the management and employees have the relevant skills, support and training to undertake works.
  • Promote safety awareness and activities on the understanding that all accidents are preventable and that a “ZERO” accident target is the Company’s aim.
  • Endeavors to ensure that all contractors are apply the same standards of health, safety and environment protection as that of the Company.
